Information about Fire

Fire is dangerous. Fire is the rapid oxidation of a material (the fuel) in the exothermic chemical process of combustion, releasing heat, light, and numerous reaction products.

In simple terms, Fire is dangerous, and it is extremely hot for us to touch.

Fire is not something humans should play with.

Lastly, Fire is not only caused naturally but it can also be committed by a person. Arson is the act of setting fire to buildings on purpose.
